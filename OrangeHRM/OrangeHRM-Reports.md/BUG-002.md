# BUG-002 : Upgrade Section Does Not Display Pricing Information

During manual testing of the OrangeHRM application, an issue was observed in the **Upgrade** section where pricing information was not displayed after selecting the upgrade option. 

Although the Call-to-Action (CTA) functioned, the expected pricing details were not presented to the user.

## Observation

When the **Upgrade** option was selected, the page opened successfully, but no pricing information was displayed for the available plans.

## Impact

The absence of pricing information may confuse users who intend to explore or purchase premium features, resulting in an incomplete upgrade experience.

## Recommendation

- Verify that pricing data is correctly retrieved and displayed.
- Ensure all available subscription plans are rendered properly.
- Display an appropriate message if pricing information is temporarily unavailable.


## Conclusion

The Upgrade section successfully opens; however, the pricing information was not displayed during testing. 

Investigating this issue would help improve the completeness and usability of the upgrade workflow.
