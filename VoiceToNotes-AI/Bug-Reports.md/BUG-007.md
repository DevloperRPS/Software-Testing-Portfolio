## Environment

* **Operating System:** Ubuntu 24.04 LTS & Android 15
* **Browser:** Google Chrome (Latest Version)
* **Platform:** Desktop
* **Testing Type:** Manual Functional Testing
* **Build/Test Date:** During QA Technical Assessment

## Severity: **Minor**

## Priority: **P2 – Medium**


## Steps to Reproduce

1. Open the VoiceToNotes.ai landing page in Google Chrome.
2. Browse through different sections of the landing page and navigate to the **Blog** page.
3. Observe the loading behavior of images.
4. Refresh the page if necessary and compare the image loading behavior.


## Expected Result

All images on the landing page and blog should load consistently during the initial page load without requiring any manual refresh.


## Actual Result

Several images on the landing page and blog either load slowly or fail to appear initially. In some cases, refreshing the page is required before the images are displayed correctly.


## Conclusion

Image loading on the landing page and blog is inconsistent, resulting in delayed or missing visual content during the initial page load. 

Optimizing image delivery, caching, and asset loading would improve page performance and provide a smoother user experience.
